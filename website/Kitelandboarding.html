KITE LANDBOARDING





Kite landboarding is a sport in which the rider is pulled on the ground (often a beach) by a kite. It is a great option for kitesurfers who live in areas where the wind is low and still want to fly their kites. Kite landboarding is very dangerous, so wearing protective gears is highly advised, especially if you intend to perform tricks.


Landboards have different names:

  • Mountainboards
  • Groundboards
  • All terrain boards (ATB)
