KITE FOILING





Kite foiling is nearly the same as regular kiteboarding except that the rider has a hydrofoil under his feet. Although it becomes much harder to perform tricks, it allows to ride faster, sometimes even faster than 35 mph. It also brings new sensations as the board is now three feet above water.

However, it should be known that kite foiling is both harder and more dangerous than kitesurfing. Indeed, the high speed and the presence of a sharp aluminum blade below you increases the rate of accidents. Therefore, it is not advised to start kite foiling before you've reached a good level at kiteboarding.
