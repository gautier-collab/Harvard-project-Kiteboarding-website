SNOWKITING





Snowkiting, as suggested by its name, is about using a kite to glide on snow. It is very popular with kitesurfers who want to ride in Winter when the water is too cold to enjoy kiteboarding. You need less wind than on water but it also becomes more dangerous to jump.

The harness, bar and leash used in kiteboarding can also be used in snowkiting. However, instead of using inflatable kites, snowkiters rely on foil kites which are soft and cannot be damaged easily when they hit the ground. Snowkiting can be practiced either with skis or with a snowboard.
