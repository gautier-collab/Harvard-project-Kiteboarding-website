KITEBOARDING





Kiteboarding, also called kitesurfing, is a sport that relies on wind to pull a rider through the water on a board.


The minimum equipment required for kiteboarding consists in:

  • kite(s): it is highly recommended to own several kites of different sizes to be able to ride with different wind speeds
  • board (symmetrical boards are called twin tips)
  • bar with 4 or 5 lines
  • harness
  • kite leash
  • wetsuit
  • pump

There exists several styles of kiteboarding (race, wave, wakestyle), but the main ones are freeriding and freestyling. The following table helps to understand the differences between those two categories.

Kiteboarding style What is encompassed Required level Type of kite Board features Bar position
Freeriding Cruising on the surface of water Beginner Bow shape Footstraps Hooked
Freestyling Many jumps and tricks Advanced C-shape Boots Unhooked
